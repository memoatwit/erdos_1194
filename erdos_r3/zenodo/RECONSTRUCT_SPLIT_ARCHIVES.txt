The Zenodo record stores two large archives as split files to stay within
upload limits. After downloading all files into one directory, reconstruct them
with:

  cat r3-212-campaign-v1-drat-part2.tar.part-* > r3-212-campaign-v1-drat-part2.tar
  cat r3-212-campaign-v1-lrat-32735.tar.part-* > r3-212-campaign-v1-lrat-32735.tar

The reconstructed tar files correspond to the entries named
`r3-212-campaign-v1-drat-part2.tar` and
`r3-212-campaign-v1-lrat-32735.tar` in the local upload staging directory.

The other uploaded archives are unsplit:

  r3-212-campaign-v1-core.tar
  r3-212-campaign-v1-drat-part1.tar
  r3-212-campaign-v1-lrat-63231.tar

Use MANIFEST.sha256 from the core archive to verify extracted contents after
reconstructing and unpacking the split archives.
